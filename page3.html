About Us

InfoPro Solutions started its operation at Bangalore, India in 1997 by developing software products for global pharmaceutical companies. Soon, it grew to a few hundred employees. With about two decades of experience & expertise in people management and operations management, InfoPro ventured into Human Resource Management Services with staffing, Professional Employer Organization (PEO) services.

We believe the growth of any organization needs to be in the hands of a Quality Service Provider, and we being your business partner, your success is in trustable hands today and every day.
